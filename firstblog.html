How the boy who dreamed of a defense got into computer science? larger bolder red

Hello and welcome everyone to my very first blog of coding journey.

Just as every boy dreams of serving his country when he grows up and gives his participation in the development of the country, in the same way, I also decided that I will also serve my country by joining the defence. As I grew up, I started getting interested in technology as well. When I completed my schooling I saw that my friends have been preparing for IIT. I did not know anything about IIT, so I took admission in a coaching institute after completing my 10th class after taking information about IIT from my friends and started preparing because I was interested in technology. During the preparation of IIT, I came across many distractions which I could not understand because at that time I was in my teenage and I needed a guide but at that time I had no guide because of which I knew what was going to happen. Failed in my IIT exam because my preparation was not complete due to distractions. Then I thought that what I had dreamed as a child, to serve the country by joining the defense, I started preparing for the defense. During the preparation, I started working on the physical as well as written, as everyone knows that it is also necessary to be physically fit to join the defense. Preparation continued, the exam came, I gave the exam and I passed the exam, after that I started working harder on physical, that day has also come, I have also qualified physical, which I could have done by preparing. Now comes the medical test and I come to know that I am medically unfit which I cannot cure. Then I decided to choose my old computer science field, so after collecting information that how can I go to computer science field again, then I came to know about BCA and MCA that I can take admission in NIT for mca by giving nimcet exam, THEN I took admission in BCA in PATNA SCIENCE college and continued my studies, but even after that, I did not give up the stubbornness of going to defense, on the belief that I have to do something by going to the army. And I cracked the defense exam 6 times but I got failure only . I get medically unfit again and again. Then, getting frustrated, I focused on my computer science studies. And I went back to the entrance exam of NIT along with my bca studies because if I had to do computer science then I have to do it from IIT NIT only. But this time I had thought that the mistake was made while taking IIIT JEE preparation in Twelth Board, that is not to be done. And I did complete focused studies during my NIMCET preparation and after hard work I cleared the NIMCET exam and got admission in nit raipur, after many failures finally i can say its my achievement. now i can proudly say i am NITian now that was i dreamed before thanks for reading thisüôè
